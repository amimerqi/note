**这是一个矩阵（带上一个大括号的）**
$$
\begin{pmatrix}
 x_{11} & x_{12} &x_{13} &\cdots &x_{1n}\\
 x_{21}&x_{22}&x_{23}&\cdots&x_{2n}\\
 \vdots&\vdots&\vdots&\ddots&\cdots\\
 x_{n1}&x_{n2}&x_{n3}&\cdots&x_{nn}
\end{pmatrix}
$$
**这也是一个矩阵（带上一个括号的框框）**
$$
\begin{bmatrix}
x_{11}&x_{12}&x_{13}&\cdots&x_{1n}\\
x_{21}&x_{22}&x_{23}&\cdots&x_{2n}\\
\vdots&\vdots&\vdots&\ddots&\vdots\\
x_{n1}&x_{n2}&x_{n3}&\cdots&x_{nn}    
\end{bmatrix}
$$
